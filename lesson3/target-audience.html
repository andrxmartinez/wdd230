Target Audience

As previously mentioned in the website's purpose, Reborn chamber is meant to help every individual or business to establish connections with other that can help and support. Based on that, our Target audience is very broad, as it could help a single individual from any age, as well as a big organization. No age limits are set, we would like everyone to have this opportunity, regardless of their age or profession.
